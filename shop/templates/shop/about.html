About HARD COPY

Hard Copy is an online vinyl record store, based in Ireland but shipping all over Europe. We stock a wide variety of vinyl records from classic collectibles to modern releases. Catering for all tastes, from classic rock to modern techno. Customers of all walks of life will find their music here, from a sunday morning listener to a session DJ.

We also stock merchandise from different artists and a small collection of high quality audio equipment to help you get the best out of this beautiful format that is vinyl. Have a look around the store we are sure you will fnd something you like!

About US

The store was set up and launched by Frank and Tom, two life long listeners, lovers and collectors of vinyl records. With experience in retail and audio engineering, between them they made the perfect business partners to take on this project. With their passion for vinyl, they wanted to make a one stop shop for anyone else sharing that love or people looking to start their vinyl journey for their frst time. They have an eclectic taste for music, and wanted the store to represent that.

The team here at HARD COPY, prioritise good customer care over anything else. We hope this comes through in the service you recieve. Any queries you have please get in touch.

About The Site

The web store we have built here, aims to make the purchasing proccess as simple as posible. From the home page, a list of all our products are availible, for browsing. The products can be sorted and filtered by category, music genre, or product type. At the top of each page a search bar is available making it easy to find the exact product you are looking for.

Each product has a review section where customers can leave their review of the product helping to inform or future purchasers. As a user of the site you can register for an account, ggiving you the ability to create wishlists of items you like but are not ready to purchase yet, write reviews of products / albums, and save you checkout details to make future purchases even easier.
